BEHIND THE APP

The Calorie Calculator is based on the Mifflin - St Jeor equation. It allows you to find your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) ,aka the amount of energy needed for your body to support only its basic vital functions. Biologically, the Basal Metabolic Rate are different for Men and Women.

This calculation consists of getting your sex, age, height, weight and how often do you engage in physical activity.
